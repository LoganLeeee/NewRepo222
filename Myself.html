About Myself

My name is Li Xinlin. I am from the northern province of Liaoning, China. I am a computer graduate student at Australian National University.

I graduated from Huazhong University of Science and Technology with a bachelor degree in mechanical engineering.

I like mechanical engineering very much, and I am interested in computers at the same time, because I find that today to automate production, many problems depend on computer science to solve it.

At the same time, the computer will have more room for development in the future.

Although my computer foundation is not so good, I hope to enhance it through my own efforts and study.
